About Us

JumpUp Tutoring is a group run by students in sixth form who volunteer to help students taking their GCSE exams by offering free, high-quality tuition. All learning is virtual, involving tutors and students working closely together to identify strengths and weaknesses, and taking targeted actions to strengthen weak points, thereby ensuring they have the confidence to perform well in their exams.

Safeguarding

The safety and wellbeing of our students is our absolute top priority. We are committed to creating a secure, supportive, and respectful learning environment where every student feels comfortable and protected.

Our Commitment: All tutoring sessions are conducted with the highest standards of safeguarding in mind. We maintain professional boundaries and ensure that all interactions are appropriate and focused on academic support.

Open Communication: We encourage open dialogue between students, parents, and our tutoring team. If any student or parent has concerns about their tutoring experience, we urge you to speak with us immediately.

Your Voice Matters: Students should always feel empowered to raise any concerns, no matter how small they may seem. We are here to listen, support, and take appropriate action to ensure every student's safety and comfort throughout their learning journey.

Continuous Improvement: We regularly review our safeguarding practices and welcome feedback to help us maintain the highest standards of student care and protection.
