About Us

JumpUp Tutoring is a group run by students in sixth form who volunteer to help students taking their GCSE/SAT/11+ exams by offering free, high-quality tuition. All learning is virtual, involving tutors and students working closely together to identify strengths and weaknesses, and taking targeted actions to strengthen weak points, thereby ensuring they have the confidence to perform well in their exams.
